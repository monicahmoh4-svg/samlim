Investor View

Investment case, traction metrics and impact thesis for DFIs and impact investors.

Why SAM-LiMP is investment-ready
Africa's agri-food market is projected to reach $1 trillion by 2030. SAM-LiMP captures value at the farm-gate — the most underserved, highest-margin point in the chain. Our model is asset-light (we don't own hubs), transaction-based, and network-effect driven. Every new farmer and hub makes the platform exponentially more valuable for everyone.
